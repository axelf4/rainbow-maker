Rainbow Maker

Is your document dull? Is the text making you throw up? Or do you just wish you could have more rainbows?

Rainbow Maker is a Google Docs add-on that solves all these problems and more, by giving you the option of stylizing your everyday text. After just a few weeks of use, you'll never get that sweet rainbow taste of your tongue. Get it from here.

Previously I had to go through the hassle of making my own rainbows. — William J.

Privacy Policy

Rainbow Maker or its affiliates do NOT, in any way, store or collect any private user data, with the exception of user privided options.

Frequently asked questions

Rainbow Maker won't show up?

Unfortunately this is a problem with Google Docs and nothing I can fix. Try giving it some time or refreshing the page.

There are now a bajillion colors among my custom colors!

Rainbow Maker adds color to the page so this is unavoidable. However, revert the text color and the colors will go away.

Is it open source?

Yes, the source code is hosted on GitHub under the GPLv3 license.
