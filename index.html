Rainbow Maker

Is your document dull? Is the text making you throw up? Or do you just wish you could have more rainbows?

Rainbow Maker is a Google Docs add-on that solves all these problems and more, by giving you the option of stylizing your everyday text. After just a few weeks of use, you'll never get that sweet rainbow taste of your tongue. Get it from here.

Previously I had to go through the hassle of making my own rainbows. — William J.

Privacy Policy

Rainbow Maker does NOT, in any way, collect user data. We need access to your current document only to change the color of your selection, nothing more; nothing less. No text is stored anywhere, nor is it read by anyone other than possibly you. Usage of Rainbow Maker is completely anonymous. If you would desire, you are free to access the source code and make your own copy of the software, provided you comply with the license.

Google

Our add-ons for Google Docs exist on Google's servers. You need a Google-account to use our add-ons and through doing that you agree to their terms of usage.

Third-party sources

There are none.

Safety

To protect the little of your information that we process we ensure reasonable precautions and follow industry best practices.

Storage

The settings of the add-on are stored on Google's servers through the PropertiesService API. Only you have access to your stored settings.

Cookies

We do NOT store any cookies. The software doesn't even eat cookies. I reckon it is allergic.

Changes to this privacy policy

We do NOT reserve the right to change this privacy policy ever.

Frequently asked questions

Rainbow Maker won't show up?

Unfortunately this is a problem with Google Docs and nothing I can fix. Try giving it some time or refreshing the page.

There are now a bajillion colors among my custom colors!

Rainbow Maker adds color to the page so this is unavoidable. However, revert the text color and the colors will go away.

Is it open source?

Yes, the source code is hosted on GitHub under the GPLv3 license.
